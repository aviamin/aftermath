This is the third training run (v3). v1 (no augmentation/normalization)
overfit hard (macro-F1 0.297, val loss bottomed at epoch 2 then rose every
epoch after). v2 added augmentation + normalization + weight decay: a much
healthier training curve but a WORSE held-out macro-F1 (0.272), especially
on the minority classes -- weight decay was the suspected culprit. This run
(v3) keeps augmentation + normalization but drops weight decay to 0, to
isolate whether that combination gets a healthy curve without sacrificing
macro-F1. Result: 15 epochs (vs. v1's 8), best val loss 0.7240 @ epoch 9
(the best of all three runs), train/val gap of only 0.18 at the final
epoch -- essentially matching v2's training-curve health. See
notebooks/04_evaluation.ipynb for the resulting held-out macro-F1.
